ChatHTML alpha privacy summary

Signed-in sessions, messages, uploaded files, account identifiers, authentication sessions, and managed-usage records are stored to provide the service. Passwords are stored as salted password hashes; provider credentials used by the managed service are not sent to the browser.

Managed prompts are processed by OpenRouter and the selected model provider. Search or retrieval features can contact configured search providers and requested public websites. Browser-direct requests use the provider selected by the user.

Bug reports are stored privately by ChatHTML support. They include the submitted text, session title, page path, browser information, viewport, and only images explicitly added by the user. Do not submit secrets.

Users can export or permanently delete their account data from Profile settings. Account chats and files are retained until the user deletes them or the account. Authentication sessions expire after 30 days. Managed-request history is retained for 90 days; aggregate usage debt is retained until account deletion. Private bug reports are retained for 30 days. Encrypted backups are retained for 14 days, so deleted data can remain in a protected backup until that backup expires.

For support or a privacy request, use the in-app Bug Report dialog.
