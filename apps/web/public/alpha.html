ChatHTML alpha notice

ChatHTML is experimental alpha software. Features may fail, responses may be inaccurate, and data may be lost. Keep independent copies of important work.

Managed mode

When you use the managed model, prompts and necessary attachments are sent through ChatHTML Service to OpenRouter and the selected model provider. Search and retrieval may send queries or public URLs to the configured search provider.

Browser-direct mode

A user-provided model key is kept only for the current browser session and requests go from the browser to that provider. Local chats are browser data and can disappear when site data is cleared or the device is lost.

Managed usage is subject to the rolling allowance shown in account settings. Alpha access and limits may change.
